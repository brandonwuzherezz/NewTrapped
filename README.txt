Welcome to the Alpha version of Trapped.

Controls
------------------------
F to activate flashlight
Mouse to move flashlight
WASD to move

Goal/Objective/Obstacles
------------------------
Find your way through the maze to get to the blue light.
Shine your flashlight on shadows to open up pathways. 
Use your light sparingly as you only have it for a few 
seconds. If you find yourself without light. Pick up batteries
to regenerate light and press F to reactivate.

KNOW BUGS
------------------------
Only way to escape game to to alt tab and quit exe at the moment.
Only runs in Ultra and High Graphics
